  ### ⚙️ About My Automation Work

  My approach to automation is centered on maintainability and reliability. Rather than simply increasing test counts, I
   prioritize the creation of stable, modular frameworks that minimize flakiness and provide immediate, actionable
  feedback to development teams. I believe the role of an SDET is to accelerate the release cycle by building a safety
  net that catches regressions without slowing down the velocity of the engineering team.
